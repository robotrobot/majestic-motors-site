Featured project:

Morgan Plus 4

Part 1: Collection and stripping

December 2019

We turned up on a bright but brisk winter day to extract the Morgan from the barn it had sat in since 1978 - partly dismantled but complete. Given the barn was situated down an extremely muddy track, which we had to dig and line with wood chippings to allow our tow vehicle to access, it did take us most of the afternoon. Tea, chocolate biscuits and the prospects of getting stuck into the project kept our spirits high!

Once back at the workshop we started the long process of fully stripping the car of all its components, and carefully photographing and documenting each component.
