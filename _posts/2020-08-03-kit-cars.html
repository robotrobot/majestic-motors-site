Kit car projects

Majestic Motors can help you with full or part build of kitcars and self-build projects. If you are building the vehicle yourself but lack skills or confidence for that tricky task, we are on hand to offer friendly advice, carry out part, or all of the work. We can also complete a safety check of work carried out by you if you just want that added piece of mind.
