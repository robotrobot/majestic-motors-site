Featured Project: Alfa Romeo GTV2000

Once owner since 1974

Full bare metal restoration for original keeper

This Alfa Romeo has been with the owner for over 46 years. The car unfortunately needed some major surgery with extensive rust in the floors, bulkhead, jacking points sills and wings.

After a nut and bolt rebuild to better than new condition, the car will be returned to the owner very shortly.
