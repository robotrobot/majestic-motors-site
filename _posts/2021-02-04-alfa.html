Marque Focus: Alfa

Servicing, repair, recommissioning and restoration

Alfa Romeo is one of our favourite marques here at Majestic Motors - especially the 105 series. Between us, we own three of them! It's also a popular make amongst our customers, over the last couple of years not a week has gone by without one being in the workshop!

We can offer simple servicing, through mechanical recomissioning or overhaul, to full body restoration and trim.

Below you'll find a small sample of the project work we have completed recently:

  • Alfa GTV 2000 full restoration
  • Alfa Spider 2000 reassembly
  • Alfa 2600 Sprint
  • Alfa GT Junior restomod with Twin Spark engine conversion

Alfa GTV 2000 full restoration

This 2000 GTV is a one-owner car, we fully bare-metal restored it for the original owner over a period of three years.

Alfa Spider 2000 reassembly

This car arrived to us with body and paintwork finished, but disassembled in a number of boxes. It had been through a number of specialists in the past, who had failed to complete the project. The owner asked us to put the jigsaw puzzle back together. There were many missing pieces!

The end result is a car that drives exceptionally well and the owner can use and enjoy.

Alfa 2600 Sprint

We're currently recomissioning "Georgio", an exceptionally rare UK-market RHD 2600 Sprint, to his former glory. His immediate needs are in the cabin - his tired front seats, dash top and door cards need some attention.

Alfa GT Junior restomod with Twin Spark engine conversion

This car is one of ours, which we have been developing for road and track use over the last few years.

Equipped with a fully-forged Twin Spark engine conversion on throttle bodies producing over 200bhp, mated to a full race clutch and Gripper LSD rear axle.

We have developed a motorsport electronics package, adding both reliability and technology in the form of full datalogging capability with track camera system.

We are further refining the package during 2021 with the addition of an AP Racing floating disc conversion.

Despite the race car technology the car also features a leather interior with wool carpeting and heated seats! The car is an example of the kind of engineering we can over to any restomod project.
