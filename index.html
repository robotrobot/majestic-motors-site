Classic car & motorbike restoration, modification and customisation

Based near Birmingham, Coventry, Warwick, Oxford, Leamington Spa, Banbury and the Cotswolds

We specialise in classic cars & motorbikes and offer a range of services to preserve, restore or enhance your pride & joy. We can also help with kit cars, race preparation and design/manufacturing of replacement parts & components.

What makes Majestic Motors unique is that we refuse to cut corners but instead take the time to find the root cause of the fault and fix the problem properly. Our engineering background gives us a solid foundation and logical professional approach, combined with the ability to think around the problem, outside the box.

Attention to detail is paramount and we pride ourselves in ensuring the job is done right.
