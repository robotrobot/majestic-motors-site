Meet the team

Ben Thompson

Technical Director

Ben started Majestic Motors in 2015 after a 15-year career in the automotive industry as a Design and Production Engineer, working for iconic brands such as Triumph Motorcycles, Aston Martin, Marcos, TVR and Caterham.

You can read more about Ben's experience here.

At Majestic Motors, Ben heads up the workshop side of the business, supporting technicians and overseeing quality, designing and engineering custom components and builds as well as estimating jobs and diagnosing and assessing vehicles.

Ben has an eclectic collection of classic cars, most - truth be told - in need of full restoration. His favourite marque is Mercedes Benz - between them he and his partner own a pair of Mercedes SLs, one classic one modern!

Chris Clarke

Commercial Director

Chris had a successful career building and developing software companies before launching Majestic Motorsport with Ben in 2017. Following a successful couple of years managing the race team, Chris became a partner in the restoration and storage businesses in early 2020.

Chris leads on sales, marketing, business development and customer services across all three businesses.

Being a child of the 1980s, Chris' favourite marque is Porsche, although he also has a soft spot for Alfa Romeo and has been slowly "improving" his 1972 Alfa Romeo GT Junior for about 15 years.

Lisa is partial to a convertible and currently drives a Mazda MX-5 (Mk3) with an extremely fetching duck tail spoiler.
